Who We Are

Of Colour is more than a directory — it is a movement rooted in dignity, belonging, and the belief that every person deserves an equal chance to thrive.

Our Story

Of Colour was born from a simple truth: that talent, skill, and entrepreneurial spirit are evenly distributed — but opportunity is not. Founded under Blueprints of You, a registered NGO, we exist to close that gap.

We created a social development fund that fuels outreach programs in disadvantaged communities, giving small business owners a credible platform and an amplified voice. When you hire through Of Colour, you're not just finding a service — you're investing in someone's livelihood and your community.

Every business listed on our platform is personally verified and supported through training, promotion, and community advocacy. We don't just list businesses — we build them up.

"Ubuntu — I am because we are."
